TIFF tools

LibTiff.Net, the managed version of the LibTiff library for reading and writing TIFF.

This software distribution comes with a small collection of programs for TIFF images. Some of these tools are useful in their own right. Many of them however are more intended to serve as programming examples for using the TIFF library.

NameDescription
Tiff2Pdf Converts TIFF images to PDF.
Tiff2Rgba Converts a TIFF image to RGBA color space.
TiffCP Copy, concatenate, and convert TIFF images (e.g. switching from LZW-compressed to uncompressed).
